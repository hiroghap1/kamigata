About Premium Seat

“Premium Seat is now available!”

“Don’t you want to enjoy Kamigata Nihonshu World in a “more relaxed,” “more comfortable,” and “more luxurious” manner than usual? And not being affected by the weather?”

  • ☆We will prepare exclusive tents, tables and chairs on the stage located in the south of the venue. Please have a seat and enjoy foods and drinks.
  • ☆The staff from well-known Osaka izakayas (May 4: Kan no Miho, May 5: Nihonshu Usagi) will serve you in person. You can ask them what foods and drinks to choose, of course.
  • ☆Please be seated while you order and wait. The staff will bring you foods and drinks to your table.
  • ☆Please pay the check all together (a seat charge, foods and drinks) when you leave the seat.

◎ Charge (90 minutes)

Please buy an entrance ticket for the venue as well.

  • Adults
    1000 yen
  • Minors
    500 yen
  • Preschool children
    free of charge

  • Basically the seat is reserved for 90 minutes, but you may extend the time if there are available seats (extension charge: 300 yen for adults and 150 yen for minors). The seats will be closed at 16:00.
