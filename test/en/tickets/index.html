How to buy a ticket

There are 3 ways to buy a ticket!

  • Buy an advance ticket directly from nearby participating restaurants, izakayas, shops or consignment shops.
    The price is 700 yen, which is the most reasonable among all tickets.
    You might be able to buy it even when the shops are closed. Please feel free to contact the shops. Click here to see the list of restaurants where you can buy a ticket.
  • e+Buy an advance ticket at e+ (e-plus).
    The price is 800 yen. You can easily buy online from anywhere, anytime.
    You may also use “Sma-Ticket (smart ticket)” so that you only need your smartphone at the entry without a printed ticket.
    Click here to buy a ticket at e+
  • Buy a ticket at the door.
    The price is 1000 yen. We will start selling tickets from 9:30 AM at the reception.
    We may stop selling tickets depending on the degree of congestion in the venue.
    The special glass with Sakazukin will be replaced by a plastic cup if it becomes sold-out.

Attention

  • The ticket is a two-day combination ticket of May 4 and 5.
  • Advance tickets are on sale until May 3.
  • Please do bring the special glass that you will get on May 4 in exchange for the ticket on the second day, May 5, as well. The glass replaces the ticket as the admission ticket.
